General Information

Our restaurant is located at 407 Ormeau Road, BT7 3GP,Belfast, Northern Ireland.

We are open Tuesday to Sunday from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM, We are closed on Mondays.

You can make a reservation by calling us at +44 743 641 8993, or via booking , or through our social media pages Facebook or Instagram.

Menu and Food

We specialize in authentic Nigerian cuisine, offering a variety of traditional dishes such as Jollof Rice, Suya, Egusi Soup, and Pounded Yam.

Yes, we have a selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Please let our staff know about your dietary preferences, and we will be happy to assist you.

Some of our dishes are spicy, but we can adjust the spice level to your preference. Just let us know when you place your order.

Yes, we offer several gluten-free dishes. Please inform our staff about your dietary requirements, and we will recommend suitable options.

Services

Yes, we offer both takeaway and delivery services. You can place your order by calling us, ordering through our website, or using food delivery apps like Uber Eats, Just Eats and Deliveroo.

Yes, we provide catering services for events and private parties. Please contact us at +44 743 641 8993 for more information and to discuss your requirements.

Yes, we can accommodate large groups. Please make a reservation in advance to ensure we can prepare adequately for your party.

Payment and Policies

We accept cash, debit/credit cards, and contactless payments. For online orders, we also accept payments through various digital wallets and payment platforms.

We kindly ask that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel your reservation.

Our dress code is casual. We want our guests to feel comfortable while enjoying their meal.
