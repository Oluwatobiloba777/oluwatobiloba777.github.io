About Us

Welcome to MamaBobo Restaurant

MamaBobo Restaurant is a proudly family-run establishment founded by a mother and son who relocated to Belfast from Nigeria over 20 years ago. What began as a journey of hope has grown into one of Belfast’s most loved spots for authentic West African cuisine. With rich flavours, traditional recipes, and heartfelt service, MamaBobo has earned its place as one of the highest-rated restaurants in the city.
